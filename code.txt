# filling up gas tank at gas station
Fill up gas tank
    turn off car engine
    insert credit card at fuel pump
    select "fill up to $250" option
    Until screen displays "please lift nozzel to fuel"
        select no to all options
    select fuel grade = 87
    lift nozzel from pump
    open fuel gasket
    insert nozzel into tank opening
    Until gas is full engage nozzel
    remove nozzel from tank
    place nozzel back to original position at pump
    close the fuel casket
